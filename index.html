Covid19 Detector

COVID-19 Infection Map Generation and Diagnosis from Chest X-Ray Images

Covid19 Detector developed by researchers in IIT Patna early detection and infection map generation of Covid-19 . The best deep AI model trained over the largest X-ray dataset with 119,316 images is now available for online trials below. This is a free application with no commercial strings attached. Simply Upload one or more X-ray images and you will see the infection maps generated in few seconds like the examples shown below.

Covid19 Detector allows the users to monitor the progression of infection with time. Users can upload multiple X-Rays of the same patient at different time intervals to observe the growth or decline in infection area, like the examples shown above. Press the Upload button below to upload X-Ray(s) for real-time diagnosis.

Dataset

You can download Covid19 Detector benchmark dataset from this Link. There are 4603 COVID-19 images and the dataset includes ground-truth segmentation masks of the COVID-19 images used for infection map generation application. Total number of chest X-ray images is 119,316.
